Privacy Policy

SMART STORAGE

Effective Date: April 3, 2025

At Smart Storage, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our services. This policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Who We Are

Smart Storage is a student company operating under the legal and fiscal responsibility of Stichting Jonge Honden.

Contact Email: smart.storage.2y@gmail.com

2. What Data We Collect

  • Name and email address (for account registration and communication)
  • Login credentials (for secure access)
  • Product information you input (e.g., expiration dates)
  • Payment information (only if subscribing to paid services)
  • Technical data (e.g., IP address, browser type, access times)

3. Why We Collect Your Data

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To communicate with you about updates or support
  • To process subscriptions and payments
  • To improve user experience and functionality
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Your consent (e.g., when subscribing or submitting forms)
  • Contractual necessity (e.g., providing our services)
  • Legal obligations (e.g., tax or student company regulations)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share it only with:

  • Stichting Jonge Honden (for compliance and reporting)
  • Payment service providers (e.g., for processing transactions)
  • Educational supervisors (for monitoring the student project)

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Account data: as long as your account is active
  • Subscription and financial records: up to 7 years (as required by Dutch tax law)
  • Expired accounts or inactive users: deleted within 12 months

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correct or delete your data
  • Right to object to or restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at smart.storage.2y@gmail.com

8. Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data from unauthorized access or misuse.

9. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience. You can control or disable cookies in your browser settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. You will be notified via email or website announcement if major changes occur.
